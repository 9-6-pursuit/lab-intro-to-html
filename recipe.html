The Perfect PB&J

Description

This peanut butter and jelly sandwhich is my favorite sandwhich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right.

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • Bread (Two Slices)
  • Knife
  • Cutting Board

Directions

  1. Lay both slices of the bed next to reach other on a cutting board.
  2. With a butter knife, spread a 1/8th inch layer of peanut butter on the left side.
  3. On the right side, spread a 1/8th inch layer of jelly.
  4. Carefully place the two halves together so that the jelly is on top.
  5. Enjoy!
