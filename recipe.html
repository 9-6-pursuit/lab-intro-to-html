The Perfect PB&J

Description

This peanut butter and jelly sandwich is my favorite sandwich. It has the perfect balance of ingredient and look great when made right.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread.
  • 1 jar of grape jelly.
  • 1 jar of creamy peanut butter.
  • A butter knife.
  • A sharp knife.
  • A cutting board.

Directions

  1. Lay both slices of bread next to each other on a cutting board.
  2. With the buuter knife, spread a 1/8 inch layer of peanut butter on the left side.
  3. On the right side, spread a 1/8 inch layer of jelly.
  4. Carefully place the two halves together so that the jelly is on top.
  5. With the sharp knife, carefully cut the sandwich i half.
