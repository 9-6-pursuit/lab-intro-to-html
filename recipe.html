Charlies Famous Milksteak

Description

Charlies favorite meal from "It's Always Sunny in Philidelphia"

Ingredients

  • Whole milk (preferably from a cow)
  • Beef cut of choice
  • Jelly Beans (Optional)
  • Salt & Pepper

Directions

  1. Rub beef with salt and pepper
  2. Bring milk to a boil.
  3. Tear beef into pieces onto milk
  4. Let beef boil for at least 10 hours
  5. Remove meat from milk, let rest for 5-10 mins
  6. Serve with or without jellybeans
