The Perfect Pizza

Description

The best New York slice of pizza that you'll ever eat!

Ingredients

  • Parmesean Cheese
  • premade dough
  • premade pizza sauce
  • Basil
  • Pepperoni

Directions

  1. Kneed the dough with your hands.
  2. Spread the dough into a circle, leaving bumps at the end as crust.
  3. Apply the pizza sauce on the dough and spread the inner circle.
  4. Apply the cheese all around you pizza and then top it with your basil and pepperoni
  5. Put in oven for 30 minutes at 375 degrees farenheight then take out and cool for 5 minutes.
