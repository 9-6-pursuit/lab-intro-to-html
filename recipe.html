Cookie Recipe

Description

This is a delicious recipe that is perfect for any occasion. Here’s a bite-size rundown of how to make a cookie. Use this description as your 1-2-3 as you get ready to make your own — the standard ingredient ratio (by weight) is 1 part sugar, 2 parts fat, 3 parts flour.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup milk

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Cream together sugar and butter until light and fluffy.
  3. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  4. Sift together flour, baking powder, and salt.
  5. Add flour mixture to creamed mixture alternately with milk, beginning and ending with flour mixture.
  6. Pour batter into greased 9-inch cake pan.
  7. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until cake tester comes out clean.
