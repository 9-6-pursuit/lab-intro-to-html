Ingredients

  • Unsalted butter

Directions

  1. Boil the noodles
  2. Cook the chicken
  3. Make the Alfredo sauce
  4. Drain the pasta
  5. Put Noodles in alfredo sauce
  6. Add Chicken
  7. Top with Parmesan
