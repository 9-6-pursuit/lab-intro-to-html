The perfect PB&J

Description

This PB&J Sandwich is my favorite sandwitch. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of creamy peanut butter
  • A butter knife
  • A sharp knife
  • A cutting board

Directions

  1. Lay both slices of bread next to eachother on a cutting board.
  2. With the butter knife, spead a 1/8 inch layer of peanut butter on the left side.
  3. On the right side, 1/8 inch layer of jelly.
  4. Carefully place the 2 halves togther so that the jelly is on top.
  5. With the sharpe knife carefully cut the sandwich in half.
