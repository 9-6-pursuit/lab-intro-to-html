The Perfect Egg

Description

The sunny-side up egg is my favorite style of egg. It has the perfect texture and is a good source of protein.

Ingredients

  • 1-2 eggs
  • a slice of butter
  • all purpose seasoning
  • black pepper (optional)
  • cilantro

Directions

  1. Bring a pan over a hot stove (5-6 notches).
  2. Drop a slice of butter onto the pan and stir to cover the surface.
  3. Crack the eggs and drop the yolk onto the pan.
  4. Let the eggs cook for 25-30 seconds.
  5. Sprinkle the collection of seasonings over the top of the eggs.
  6. Turn off the stove and move the pan away from the heated area.
  7. Place a lid over the pan and let the eggs settle.
